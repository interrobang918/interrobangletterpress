yeah, well, here I am re-writing my apparently uncompelling advertisement for myself. Listening to the Velvet Underground.

Me: gainfully employed creative type with a business on the side. Born in Tulsa, raised in Minneapolis, living in Boston. I'm 37 but people guess I'm 31. I still get carded at liquor stores. Jeans & t-shirt. Blue eyes, short brown hair, beautiful smile. I play guitar, mostly acoustic these days. My other car is a bicycle. I collect books and records, do some medium format photography, and enjoy tending garden. I am a vegetarian who'll eat fish in a pinch. I take milk in my tea, cats, PBS and radio "left of the dial", cask conditioned ale, spring/summer, tattoos, backpacking, antiquing, films at The Coolidge. I tend to be wry. I do not smoke. I'm happy. I could be happier. I've been told that I'm sexy. I need a friend and a lover. Someone to renovate an old house and grow old with.

You: are confident with an inquisitive mind. You play an instrument and make art. You read books. You want to leave a legacy other than spent hydrocarbons. You want to build a nest and raise a child that is a trusting, intellegent and creative asset to society.
You have an edge. You don't cut. You wear skirts as often as overalls. You communicate openly. You do not smoke. Cigarettes. You are (censored). You listen to Mingus, Neil Young, X, Stravinsky, and The Replacements. You've been abroad. You have a life to share. Everyone has baggage, you can carry yours with one hand. You are svelte and beautiful in an unusual way. You are industrious and good with tools, and would enjoy nights doing art and music.

well, um, if any of this sounds compelling to you, ping me.
